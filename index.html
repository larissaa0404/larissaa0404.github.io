About

After I got my degree in Chemical Engineering, I knew I wanted to do more than that and I signed up for the Python course. I discovered how much I like programming and how interesting it is, with so many areas you can learn. So I continued and followed the second course in this field, JavaScript.

I discovered that I have much more patience than I thought, trying for days to discover why the code doesn't run as I would like, but still not giving up until I see the desired result. Through a lot of documentation, I realized the projects that I am going to present to you, trying to achieve different objectives that seemed important to me to know how to apply them. Although I don't have work experience, I'm sure I could handle it, because when you do it out of passion, you learn everything for pleasure. My goals are to be able to develop in a company, alongside a team and to become the best possible programmer.

Regarding my personal life, I enjoy running around the beach, hiking with my fiance, playing PS5 games, especially Fortnite, and trying different food recipes.
